GRAPHICAL CALCULATOR

How to Use Calculator

  • Enter numbers using the keypad
  • Use +, −, ×, ÷ for operations
  • AC clears the screen
  • = gives the final result
  • % for percentage calculation

This graphical calculator is designed to make your basic calculations simple and fast. You can perform addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, and percentage operations with just a single click. The clean interface ensures smooth user experience, and the real-time output makes it easy to verify your results instantly. Perfect for students, teachers, and anyone needing quick calculations!
